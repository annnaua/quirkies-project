First Faqs Trip

  • How many Quirklings in the collection?

    Each Quirkie is entitled to claim 1 Quirkling. The remaining 5.000 Quirklings will be minted via WL + Public sale to bring in a new wave of holders into Quirksville.

  • I do not have a Quirkie, can I mint a Quirkling?

    Yes! We will be offering allowlist spots to various NFT communities as well as giveaways through our Official Twitter account.

    If you miss out on an opportunity to get allowlist, you will be able to mint through the Quirklings public sale.

  • How long do I have to claim my Quirkling?

    Quirkies holders will have 60 days to claim their Quirkling.

  • When will my Quirkling be revealed?

    Quirklings will revealed 24-48 hours post mint.
